OpenBridge 5.0 beta

While version 5.0 retains the principles of the previous
OpenBridge releases, it brings numerous
enhancements to the system and a wealth of additional
content.

We've introduced a design pattern section and a new
templates section. The overall readability of the system
has been revamped, achieving a WCAG 2.1 AA rating for
both dusk and day modes and a AAA rating for the
bright palette. The maritime instruments have been
entirely overhauled with a new labeling system. We
have also overhauled the entire automation section and
revamped the alert system. Additional top bar content
has been included, such as an improved in-command
system and notifications. The icon library has been
reorganized and significantly expanded. THe integration
system has been redesigned. Finally we have added an
entirely new section for maritime augmented reality
that especially are looking to support video overlays for
ROC.
